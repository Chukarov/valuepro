Valuation Methodology

We apply internationally recognised valuation approaches and standards.

Every valuation is prepared in accordance with the International Valuation Standards (IVS) and Bulgarian legislation.

The three core approaches

Market approach

Value is determined by comparison with completed transactions for similar assets.

Cost approach

Value is based on replacement cost less accumulated depreciation.

Income approach

Value is derived from the asset's ability to generate future cash flows.
